### Fill Cell (Individual Square)

**How to use:**
1. Hover over any square
2. Orange "Fill" button appears in top-left corner
3. Click it
4. Choose vegetable, date, and plant count
5. Only that square gets filled

**Perfect for:**
- Testing new varieties
- Filling gaps
- Precise companion planting
- Individual variety trials

### Fill Column (Vertical)

**How to use:**
1. Look at column headers above the garden
2. Find the column you want (Col 1, Col 2, etc.)
3. Click the cyan "Fill Col" button
4. Choose settings
5. Entire column fills from top to bottom

**Perfect for:**
- Vertical succession planting
- Trellised crops (tomatoes, beans)
- Organizing by type
- Side-by-side variety comparison

## âš ï¸ Important Notes

### Fill Cell vs Individual Dot
- **Fill Cell**: Fills ALL dots in square with same vegetable
- **Individual Dot**: Click on single dot for different vegetable per dot
- Use Fill Cell for speed, individual dots for variety

### Overwriting
- Fill operations overwrite existing plantings
- No confirmation prompt
- Be careful when using
